Dodger

You're the little smiley face! Dodge the X as it flies at you from random directions!

This is a game for the TEC Redshift, which is a fictional video game console in the game EXAPUNKS. A standalone player for TEC Redshift games is available on Steam here.

To play the game, save the image of the cartridge to your computer and then drag it into the TEC Redshift application window.
